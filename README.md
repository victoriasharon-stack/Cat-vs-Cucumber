Features: 
1) the cucumbers fall in towards the cat from the edges of the grid or the screen, (you have to escape from them).
2) there's a scoreboard that counts how long you've survived from the cucumbers.
3) it also shows your high score, so that you'll lowkey be motivated to keep playing to try and beat the previous high score.
4) that's all ig lol
